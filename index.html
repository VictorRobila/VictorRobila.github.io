Computer Science Chats

Improving Communication in Computer Science

Goal:


The goal of Computer Science Chats is to facilitate conversations about computing and computer science designed for a high school audience. Our hope is that high school students will be able to learn through this resource and be inspired to pursue their computing dreams and show them the different careeers in computing and computer science and what they entail in terms of research, activities, and knowledge. All of our video interviews will be posted on the cschats.com website and the cschats youtube channel to create easy access to any of our materials.

Who Are We?

Victor Robila is the sole creater of Computer Science Chats. He is a high school student at Hunter College High School in New York City and has interests in various fields in Computer Science. He is curious about the different opportunities for High School Students to get into Computer Science and has started Computer Science Chats as a classroom resource to help bring awareness as to what computing and computer science entails. He has done several research projects in computer science and is well versed in C and Python.
